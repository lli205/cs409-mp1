Reading List

I’m deep in my romance era—give me slow burns, sharp banter, and just a little chaos. Recent faves include Beach Read (bookish rivals-to-lovers with real heart), A Court of Thorns and Roses (fantasy romance with high stakes and higher tension), Things We Never Got Over (grumpy–sunshine small-town comfort), and Twisted Games (forbidden royal bodyguard romance with delicious pining). If you have a rec with strong character arcs, chemistry that simmers, or a setting I can live in—send it my way!
